About 6 AM Club

Our story, our mission, our passion for cricket

Our Story

Founded in 2021(27-February-2021) the 6 AM Club began with a simple belief: champions are made in the morning. What started as a group of five passionate cricket enthusiasts meeting at dawn has grown into a thriving community of dedicated players who share a common dream.

The idea was born on a misty February morning when founder Amitlal gathered four friends at the local cricket ground. They were tired of making excusesтАФtired of saying "someday" when it came to their cricket ambitions. That morning, they made a pact: show up at 6 AM, no matter what.

Word spread quickly. Within months, more players joined, drawn by the discipline and dedication they witnessed. By the end of the first year, we had 52 committed members and had won our first local tournament. The rest, as they say, is history.
